Deep Dive: Research Skill

From stranger to personalized pitch

The research skill replaces manual Gemini/ChatGPT sessions. It produces strategic briefings with actual hooks.

Input

lead_name: "Alex Hormozi"
company: "Acquisition.com"
youtube_url: "@AlexHormozi"
context: "AI retention for course creators"

Process

  • ✓ Web search: background, news, interviews
  • ✓ YouTube scrape: recent videos + transcripts
  • ✓ Content analysis: values, philosophy, style
  • ✓ Hook generation: 3+ actionable openers

Output: Strategic Briefing

# Alex Hormozi — Account Briefing

## Philosophy
"Give away the secrets, sell the implementation"
Anti-guru, proof-focused, aggressive transparency

## Top Hooks
1. Reference "$100M Offers" framework
2. Lead with numbers (he respects data)
3. Connect to "volume negates luck"

## Don't Say
- Fluffy language, vague promises
- "Just wanted to reach out"
- Generic AI buzzwords

## Suggested Opener
"Watched your breakdown on retention curves.
Most programs we see: 15% finish rate.
We built something that changes that..."

Under the Hood

How it works

Self-hosted. Open source. Your data stays yours.

🧠

LLM Core

Claude, GPT-4, Gemini, or any provider. Switch models per-task. Use Opus for complex reasoning, Haiku for quick ops.

🔌

Plugin System

Skills are just markdown files. Drop a SKILL.md and your agent learns new capabilities instantly.

💾

Persistent Memory

MEMORY.md + daily logs. Your agent remembers context, preferences, and decisions across sessions.
